**Core Features:**

**Intelligent Query Autocomplete:** Instantly displays matching, trending Indian and global companies filtered in real-time as soon as you begin typing.
**Precision Viewport Trailing:** Includes smooth auto-scroll routing that focuses the viewport directly at the top of the newly generated intelligence report immediately upon clicking Analyze.
**Aesthetic Orbital AI Score Ring:** A fully animated gauge that dynamically wraps the investment's confidence score with slow-orbiting holographic paths, outer tech indicators, and deep ambient visual glow backdrops.
**Sector Valuation Benchmarking:** Displays company's P/E and P/B ratios alongside average sector benchmarks, with active comparison meters evaluating whether they trade at premium or discounted valuations.
**Historical Risk & Volatility Analysis (30-Day):** Automates daily mathematical returns calculations to find annualized price variance, standard deviation, average absolute daily move, and maximum peak-to-trough drawdowns accompanied by a responsive risk gauge.
**Complete Financial Scorecard:** Aggregates multi-year CAGR yields, operating ratios (ROE, ROCE), historical price charts, buy/sell technical summaries, ownership distributions, and key fundamental checkmarks.
